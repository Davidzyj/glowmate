# GlowMate Usage Guide

## Measure

Open GlowMate and use the Meter tab to check current lighting. The app estimates light locally from camera preview frames and recommends brightness, tone, distance, and direction.

## Apply A Recommendation

Tap Apply to move the recommendation into the Light tab. GlowMate saves the active settings locally and adds a local record.

## Use Fill-Light Camera

On the Meter tab, allow camera access and tap Fill-Light Camera. GlowMate opens a full-screen capture mode that uses the current recommendation's tone and brightness as screen fill light. Tap the shutter to save the photo to the system Photos library, then close the capture mode to return to Meter.

## Use Screen Soft Light

Open Light, choose brightness and color, then start the full-screen soft light. The screen becomes a warm, natural, cool, or blush light source. Tap Close to return.

## Use Presets

Open Scenes and choose a preset such as Selfie, Food, Product, Meeting, or Night Room. The preset becomes the active light setup.

## Reuse Records

Open Records to restore recent local settings. Records stay on the device.

## Language

Open Settings to choose English, Simplified Chinese, or Japanese. If no language is selected, GlowMate uses system/region inference and falls back to English.
